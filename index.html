ABOUT US

NIMAI is a shared online auction platform, proudly hosting online auctions for a wide variety
of reputable auctioneers across South Africa so that you have access to the best auction stock in the country and beyond.

With our simple bidding software, we expand your access to auctions normally off limits because of distance (be it geographical or social).

Register for a free bidder account and come see the difference for yourself.

Our platform combines cutting-edge technology with the expertise of established auctioneers, giving bidders a seamless online experience from registration to final payment. Whether you're looking for vehicles, art, electronics, or unique collectibles, NIMAI connects buyers with trusted sellers across the country. Through real-time bidding, secure payment processing, and verified listings, we ensure transparency and confidence in every transaction.
